THE PROBLEM

In Brazil, of all the trash produced, 30% has the potential to be repurposed, but only 3% actually is. The improper disposal of this waste ends up in rivers and, consequently, the ocean, as well as improvised landfills that impact public health.

OUR SOLUTION

ReClapp is a recycling app that reduces improper disposal and promotes the reuse and repurposing of waste in a fun way.

ReClapp helps you recycle your trash, learn about how to reuse materials, and share your DIYs to inspire others. All while earning points through challenges in the quest to become a sustainability master.
